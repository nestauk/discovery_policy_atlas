# **Analysis plan (draft \- to be discussed and adjusted\!)**

## **How can Policy Atlas help?**

* Overall, Policy Atlas will be helpful in **structured synthesis** of the academic and grey literature, surfacing ways (interventions) for boosting agency and resilience in different contexts. 

* It will be able to highlight **well-evidenced types of interventions**. It will also **suggest** **areas where there might be gaps** (where it doesn’t find strong studies) but those should be treated as suggestions rather than definitive arguments.

* We might also benefit from using the Policy Atlas results with **other tools such as NotebookLM** (eg, to synthesis across different searches) \- and we can also **consider quickly prototyping custom analyses** (eg, better overviews of international approaches)

* For the Policy Atlas team, this is an **opportunity to stress-test** the tool in a real-world task that’s well-suited to the tool’s purpose (generating sweeping overviews of interventions in a policy area) \- and **learn what output formats are most useful** for real users and audiences
